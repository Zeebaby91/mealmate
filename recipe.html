Jollof Rice

A popular West African dish cooked with rice, tomatoes, and spices.

Ingredients:

  • 2 cups rice
  • 4 tomatoes
  • 2 red peppers
  • 2 onions
  • Seasonings & oil

Steps:

  1. Wash the rice and set aside.
  2. Blend tomatoes, peppers, and onions into a sauce.
  3. Fry sauce in oil,add the pepper mixture
  4. Ad seasonings and stock.
  5. Add rice and cook until soft.

Fried Rice

A colorful and tasty dish mixed with vegetables and liver.

Ingredients:

  • 2 cups rice
  • sliced Carrots, peas,green pepper, and sweet corn
  • vegetablesoil
  • Liver or chicken and chicken stock
  • Seasonings,

Steps:

  1. Parboil rice and set aside.
  2. Stir-fry vegetables in oil.
  3. Add liver/chicken and seasoning.
  4. Mix rice with the vegetables and fry together.

Pounded Yam with Egusi Soup

A Nigerian delicacy made from yam and melon seed soup.

Ingredients:

  • Yam
  • Egusi (melon seeds)
  • Palm oil
  • Vegetables
  • Meat or fish
  • Seasonings

Steps:

  1. Boil yam until soft,then pound until the yam is seedless and easily molded and you have a smooth consistency .
  2. Fry ground egusi with palm oil and onions.
  3. Add meat, a little bit of seasonings, stock, and vegetables(any of your choice).
  4. Serve hot with pounded yam.
